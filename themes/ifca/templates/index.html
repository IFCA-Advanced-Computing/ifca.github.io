Advanced Computing and e-Science

Welcome to the Advanced Computing and e-Science group web portal within the IFCA. We are part of a joint research center between the Spanish National Research Council and the University of Cantabria, offering “enhanced” Science (or e-Science) to the researchers, where they can interact through the network, sharing computing resources to support their larger needs for simulation, data storage and transfer, analysis, and collaborative work.

About us

Our research efforts are focused on:

  • Cloud Computing.
  • Grid Computing.
  • High Performance Computing.
  • Data preservation.
